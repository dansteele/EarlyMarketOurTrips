Breathtaking Scandinavia

The Adventure

Starting in Belgium this is a Heli-only trip that heads North through the spectacular wilderness of Sweden and Norway. Experience Scandinavia's idyllic coastline and tranquil forests from the air; and discover some of the world's most secluded locations.

The Experience

Kortrijk

We all meet at the airport in Kortrijk and continue to Romo Island in Denmark (landing in the grounds of our hotel).

Norwegian Coastline

This ragged coastline comprises 63,000 miles of fjords, bays and islands.

Hotels

Land in the grounds of beautiful hotels high in the mountains of southern Norway or on the lakesides of Sweden

Wilderness

Explore, with a relatively free reign, the secluded and unscathed Norweigian countryside. The opportunity to catch your own food, build fires and soak in the natural habitat.

Camping

If you should choose, you can take to the wild and explore the countryside from the ground before spending the night under the stars

Swedish Lakes

Sweden is home to around 100,000 deserted lakes, providing stunningly unique scenery for hundreds of miles.

Copenhagen

For the return leg of our trip, stop off in Denmark's capitol, a beautiful city packed with quaint cafe's and shopping.
