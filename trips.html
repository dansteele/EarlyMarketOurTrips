Our Trips

 England at its Best
Re-discover some of the most famous landmarks our country has to offer with this itinerary around the South-East. Enjoy a unique perspective on the distinctive history and culture of England by flying over London and its monuments, as well as other stunning sights such as Leeds Castle and Stonehenge. Take relaxing breaks in our hand-picked heli-friendly establishments.
Une Escapade aux Châteaux
Our French châteaux tour is a fantastic opportunity to discover the breathtaking regions of Normandy, Loire, Bourgogne and Champagne. Embark on this journey to fly over an array of stunning châteaux, as well as the historical D-day beaches and the Circuit des 24 Heures from the famous Le Mans race. Stop at our selection of exceptional heli-friendly establishments for meals, accommodation and even a wine-tasting session.
Breathtaking Scandinavia
In collaboration with the experienced Prepare2Go team, this heli-only expedition is an exceptional opportunity to discover some of the most remote and untarnished locations in Scandinavia. Embark on this adventure into the Scandinavian wilderness and enjoy astonishing scenery under the almost permanent sunlight of the Northern summer. Absorb the surrounding environment as you camp near lakes, rivers, and mountains.
