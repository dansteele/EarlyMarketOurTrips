Breathtaking Scandinavia

 

Routes

Starting in Belgium this is a Heli-only trip that heads North through the spectacular wilderness of Sweden and Norway. No ground transportation whatsoever.

 

Destination Guide

Kortrijk

We all meet at the airport in Kortrijk and continue to Romo Island in Denmark (landing in the grounds of our hotel).

Norwegian Coastline

This ragged coastline comprises 63,000 miles of fjords, bays and islands. Located near Bergen, a low strand is studded with giant bolders and the sunlight bathes endless archipelagoes where the fjords run out to the sea. Then up to the North, where the land is wild and wave battered, rising rapidly out of the sea.

Norwegian Hotel

Land in the grounds of a beautiful hotel high in the mountains of southern Norway and watch the very late sunset, beer in hand.

Norwegian Wilderness

Explore, with relative free-reign, the secluded and unscathed Norweigian countryside. The opportunity to catch your own food, build fires and soak in the natural habitat.

Copenhagen

For the return leg of our trip.
